# SF8 Video Demo Script (3 Minutes)

## Recording Instructions
- Use browser screen recording (Loom, OBS, or built-in recorder)
- Resolution: 1920x1080 minimum
- Speak clearly in English or Vietnamese
- Total duration: ~3 minutes

---

## Script

### [0:00-0:30] Dashboard Overview

**Action**: Open app, land on Dashboard
**Say**: 
"Welcome to SF8 - AI Customer Behavior Prediction for Shinhan Finance.

This dashboard shows 20 new customers analyzed using alternative data from telco, e-wallet, e-commerce, and social sources.

You can see:
- 4 stat cards: total customers, and breakdown by action - push now, nurture, hold
- The hero case highlight - our highest scoring customer
- Product distribution showing which Shinhan products are recommended
- Customer lead list with scores, recommended products, and actions"

**Show**: Scroll through dashboard, point out key sections

---

### [0:30-1:30] Hero Case Deep-Dive

**Action**: Click on hero case (Nguyễn Văn An - highest score)
**Say**:
"Let me show you our hero case - Nguyễn Văn An, a 28-year-old software engineer.

SF8 scored him at [score]/100 based on 4 dimensions:
- Partner/Channel Fit: [pcf] - high digital engagement
- Behavior Signal: [bss] - strong e-wallet and ecommerce activity
- Early Reaction: [erq] - responsive to offers
- Product Affinity: [pa] - excellent fit for cashback credit card

The AI explains: [read explanation]

Based on his high e-commerce activity and online shopping patterns, SF8 recommends the Cashback Credit Card with a 'push now' action."

**Show**: Score breakdown bars, alternative data signals, AI explanation section

---

### [1:30-2:30] What-If Simulation

**Action**: Scroll to simulation panel, select "Partner/Channel Engagement" → "High Engagement", click "Run Simulation"
**Say**:
"Now let's run a what-if simulation. What if this customer had even higher partner engagement?

Base score: [base]. After simulating high engagement: [simulated]. Delta: [delta].

This shows how SF8 can help Shinhan understand which levers to pull to improve customer acquisition outcomes.

We can simulate 3 variables:
1. Partner/channel engagement
2. Product offer terms
3. Early reaction signals"

**Show**: Simulation controls, before/after scores, delta

---

### [2:30-3:00] Export/Pitch View + Close

**Action**: Navigate to Export/Pitch tab
**Say**:
"Finally, the Export view generates a complete insight report with:
- Customer profile summary
- AI recommendation with score breakdown
- Personalized outreach note in Vietnamese
- Alternative data signal summary

This can be printed or saved as PDF for pitch presentations.

**Key point**: SF8 is a decision-support tool. Qwen explains the reasoning, but all scores and actions are computed deterministically for transparency and auditability.

Thank you. SF8 - turning alternative data into customer insight for Shinhan Finance."

**Show**: Export card with all sections, click Print button briefly
